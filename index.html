About me

My name is Fred Johnstone and welcome to my portfolio. My journey began half way through the first Lockdown of 2020 in Cambridge UK when I decided (like many others I’m sure) to try making video games. This idea eventually “fizzeled” out as it were but my interest was peaked with coding.
My main strength is working with people, and I try to channel that into the websites I make, making them readable, and engaging for targeted audiences. I also firmly belive that communication is key to a good working team and I strive to bring that mindset to evey project that I participate in.
